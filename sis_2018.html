About

Step into STEM

Step into STEM is an annual conference hosted by UW WiSTEM to educate high school students on the opportunities available in STEM and provide them with a network and resources to one day pursue these fields. The conference includes hands-on workshops, student panels, STEM Professional speakers and keynotes. From coding to conducting science experiments in a lab, students are immersed into the exciting world of STEM for a day. Students preparing to leave high school are often unsure of the path they wish to take and this event is an outlet for students to consider the various possibilities within science, technology, engineering, and math.

STEP INTO STEM 2020

On October 13th, 2018 WiSTEM hosted our first Step into STEM event in partnership with Accenture. 100 students attended from highschools across Ontario.
